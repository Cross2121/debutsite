Star Oaks Group Home Star Oaks is a dedicated group home providing a supportive and nurturing environment for individuals recovering from mental health challenges and substance addiction. Our mission is to offer a safe haven where residents can receive comprehensive care, build life skills, and work towards sustainable recovery. Services Offered: 1. Personalized Care Plans: Each resident receives a tailored care plan that addresses their unique needs, combining mental health support and addiction treatment. 2. Therapeutic Interventions: Our experienced staff provides a range of therapeutic services, including individual counseling, group therapy, and specialized addiction treatment programs. 3. Life Skills Training: We focus on helping residents develop essential life skills, such as financial management, job readiness, and social skills, to facilitate their reintegration into society. 4. 24/7 Support: With around-the-clock care, we ensure that our residents have access to the support they need at any time. 5. Community Integration: We promote community involvement through various activities and partnerships, helping residents build a supportive network outside the home. At Star Oaks, we are committed to fostering an environment of hope and healing, empowering our residents to overcome their challenges and lead fulfilling lives.
